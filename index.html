FAQ

Where are your products sourced from?

We source our plants from trusted growers who use sustainable and eco-friendly practices.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! CozySprout gift cards are the perfect gift for plant lovers. You can purchase them directly on our website.

How do I care for my new plant?

Each plant comes with a care guide tailored to its needs. General tips include providing the right amount of sunlight, watering when the soil feels dry, and ensuring good drainage.

Are your plants pet-friendly?

We offer a selection of pet-friendly plants. Look for the "pet-safe" label in the product description.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days, and shipping typically takes 5-7 business days depending on your location.
